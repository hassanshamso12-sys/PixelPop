About PixelPop

Learn about our high-precision custom fabrication pipeline, active 3D printing farm, and bio-friendly materials.

🔬

0.16mm Layer Precision

We configure every print job with ultra-fine layer resolution, ensuring smooth surface finishes and structural stability for Vases, Prop Wearables, and articulating structures.

🏭

Active Printer Array

Our lab hosts over 12 high-speed Cartesian and Delta 3D printers operating on calibrated profiles to deliver short turnaround times with minimized print failure rates.

🌱

Eco-Friendly Materials

We print using non-toxic, biodegradable PLA (Polylactic Acid) derived from renewable organic sources like cornstarch, and durable, waterproof PETG for functional gadgets.
